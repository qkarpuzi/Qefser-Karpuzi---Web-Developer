A detailed look into one of my projects.

FurniCraft - Dine Eksperter på Skreddersydde Møbler This webpage showcases FurniCraft, a company specializing in custom furniture solutions. The hero section introduces the company with a compelling background image and slogan, "Vi skaper ditt drømmehus etter mål." The site features sections on their services, including complete renovations and professional team details, as well as an "About Us" section with company information. The feedback section highlights customer testimonials, and the contact section provides a form for inquiries along with contact details. The design is sleek with a modern color palette, and responsive adjustments ensure a great viewing experience on all devices.


Tools Used: Tools Used HTML5 - The core structure and content of the website are built using HTML5, providing a semantic and organized layout. CSS3 - The styling and layout of the website are managed with CSS3, including responsive design and modern styling features such as flexbox and grid systems. Media Queries - Used for creating responsive designs that adjust to various screen sizes, enhancing user experience across devices. Google Fonts - Custom fonts like 'Playfair Display' and 'Raleway' are used to enhance typography and visual appeal. Image URLs - High-quality images from sources like Freepik, Pixabay, and other image repositories are used to visually represent services and content.

This project demonstrates my skills in web development, focusing on user interaction and responsive design.
